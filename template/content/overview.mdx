# Overview

Welcome. This portal is the single place for everything we share with you —
curated, current, and organized by section. It replaces attachments scattered
across email threads.

A few things worth knowing:

- **Your link is your access.** The link you received identifies your
  organization; anyone on your team can use it. Please don't forward it outside
  your organization.
- **Sections marked "on request"** exist but open at a later stage. Ask and we
  can usually make them available same-day.
- **Documents download from the portal**, so you always get the latest version.

> This is the template's sample content. Replace every file in `content/` with
> your own material — each section in `lib/sections.ts` renders its matching
> `content/<slug>.mdx`. Images go through the `MEDIA` map, e.g.
> `<Figure media="hero" alt="…" caption="…" />` — never a raw URL.
